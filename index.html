About Proend

Excellence in Electrical Solutions

At Proend, we are committed to delivering top quality electrical products that redefine industry standards. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to create electrical solutions that not only perform exceptionally but also ensure the safety and security of your home and loved ones.

Our comprehensive range includes premium wires, LED strip light profiles, electrical pipes, fan boxes, module boxes, flood lights, and specialized electrical fittings.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to innovation, quality craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction. We don't just manufacture electrical products – we engineer peace of mind.
